Our Programs

When the pandemic hit in 2020, we at STEM Made Fun noticed that many students would face a gap in their STEM education leading us to launch our Summer Program in 2020. Since then, SMF has expanded our initiatives, spreading STEM education to a diverse array of individuals from different backgrounds. Each SMF program is unique, carrying its own story and driving transformative outcomes.
